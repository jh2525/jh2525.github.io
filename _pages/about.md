I am an Integrated M.S./Ph.D. student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at [Korea University](https://www.korea.ac.kr/), advised by Prof. Byung-Jun Lee. My research centers on **reinforcement learning** — including offline RL, diffusion-based planning, and multi-agent RL — as well as preference optimization and safety for large generative models.

Feel free to reach out by email. You can also find my work on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=mklwOMMAAAAJ) and [GitHub](https://github.com/jh2525).
